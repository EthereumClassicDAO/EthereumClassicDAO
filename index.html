What is Olympia?

The Olympia Upgrade (ECIP-1111) integrates EIP-1559 and introduces a protocol-level treasury and DAO governance — aligning ETC with the modern EVM ecosystem.

EIP-1559

Modern fee mechanism with base fees, tips, and predictable gas pricing.

Olympia Treasury

Base fees fund a transparent treasury to support ETC development and growth.

Olympia DAO

On-chain governance system to allocate funds through open proposals and voting.

Protocol-Level Funding

ETC's first self-sustaining treasury model. From each transaction, a portion of fees goes to fund public goods, infrastructure, and protocol development — transparently and on-chain.

Olympia DAO Governance

Community members propose and vote on Ethereum Classic Funding Proposals (ECFPs). All decisions are open, auditable, and governed on-chain through transparent mechanisms.

Our Mission

To make Ethereum Classic the most sustainable and secure smart contract blockchain under Proof-of-Work. Upholding the values of immutability, decentralization, and sound money.
